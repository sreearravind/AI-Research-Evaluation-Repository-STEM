# CONTRIBUTOR AGREEMENT

## 1. Purpose

This Contributor Agreement defines the responsibilities, rights, and expectations of individuals submitting content to this repository.

By submitting a contribution, you agree to comply with the policies, standards, and ethical requirements outlined in this document.

---

## 2. Nature of the Repository

This repository is:

* an open scientific archive
* a dataset for studying AI-generated scientific reasoning
* not a peer-reviewed journal

All submissions are considered **AI-generated or AI-assisted research artifacts**.

---

## 3. Contributor Eligibility

Submissions are accepted from:

* Students
* Academic researchers
* Industry professionals
* Independent contributors in STEM fields

---

## 4. Contributor Roles

Each submission may include one or more of the following roles:

### 4.1 Prompt Architect

Designs the prompts used to generate the AI output.

### 4.2 Research Initiator

Defines the research problem, hypothesis, or objective.

### 4.3 Model Operator

Executes the prompts using AI systems.

### 4.4 Validation Reviewer (Optional)

Verifies references, results, and claims.

---

## 5. Contributor Responsibilities

By submitting content, contributors agree to:

### 5.1 Transparency

* Fully disclose prompts used
* Provide complete AI output without modification
* Declare model details and parameters

### 5.2 Accuracy and Honesty

* Clearly distinguish verified and unverified content
* Report hallucinations honestly
* Avoid misleading claims

### 5.3 Ethical Compliance

* Do not include confidential or proprietary data
* Do not include copyrighted material without permission
* Avoid sensitive or restricted information

### 5.4 Reproducibility

* Provide sufficient details for replication
* Follow the submission template strictly

---

## 6. AI Disclosure Requirement

All submissions must:

* Clearly state AI involvement
* Avoid presenting AI-generated content as human-authored research
* Include prompt and generation context

Failure to disclose AI usage may result in removal of the submission.

---

## 7. Data and Content Policy

Contributors must ensure that:

* data sources are properly cited
* licensing conditions are respected
* public or open datasets are preferred

Prohibited content includes:

* proprietary datasets
* confidential industrial data
* restricted or classified information

---

## 8. Intellectual Credit

The repository recognizes contributor roles as:

* conceptual contributors (idea, prompt design)
* operational contributors (model execution)
* validation contributors (verification)

Each submission will include metadata to preserve contributor credit.

---

## 9. License and Usage

By submitting content, contributors agree that:

* their submission will be publicly accessible
* the content may be used for research, benchmarking, and analysis
* proper attribution will be maintained

The repository license will govern reuse and distribution.

---

## 10. Disclaimer of Scientific Validity

Contributors acknowledge that:

* submissions are not peer-reviewed
* results may contain errors or hallucinations
* independent validation is required before real-world use

---

## 11. Moderation and Removal

Repository maintainers reserve the right to:

* reject submissions that do not meet guidelines
* request modifications
* remove content that violates policies

---

## 12. Agreement Confirmation

By submitting a contribution, you confirm that:

* you have read and understood this agreement
* your submission complies with all policies
* you accept responsibility for the content provided

---

## 13. Versioning

This agreement may be updated periodically.

Contributors are encouraged to review the latest version before submitting new content.

---

## FINAL NOTE

This repository is built on:

* transparency
* scientific integrity
* collaborative exploration of AI in STEM

Your contribution plays a role in shaping the future of AI-assisted scientific research.
